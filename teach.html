Teaching: What's it like?

Over 100 students participate in SUMMIT each year. In the past, SUMMIT classes have been led by students, housefellows, CMU staff, student organizations, and Pittsburgh community members. Teaching a SUMMIT class is a great way to share any skills or interests you have with interested students! Fill out this form to teach!

Individuals
