Important Registration Information

This year, Summit will be limited to only 50 participants (excluding participants that sign-up for Survivor Support Network Training) so register today to reserve your space! After 50 participants have registered, any additional registrations will be notified and placed on a wait list.

Summit 2016 Timeline:

Fri. Jan 8: Summit participants evening check-in (dinner provided)

Sat. Jan 9: Morning and Afternoon Summit activities (breakfast, lunch, and dinner provided)

Sun. Jan 10: Only Morning Summit activities (breakfast and lunch provided)
